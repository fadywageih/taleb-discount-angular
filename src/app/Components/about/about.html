The Story Behind StudentDiscount

Built by students, for students

Taleb Discount was born out of a shared struggle to afford the rising cost of educational supplies. Six co-founders came together with one mission: to make learning more affordable by connecting students to real, verified discounts.

We're not just a website. We're a growing community of students and vendors working to make student life easier. From sketchbooks to lab coats, our platform helps you find what you need — for less — based on your major and level.

تأسس موقع طالب ديسكاونت من معاناة مشتركة في مواجهة الارتفاع المستمر في تكاليف المستلزمات الدراسية. اجتمع ستة مؤسسين طلاب على هدف واحد: جعل التعلّم أكثر توفيرًا من خلال ربط الطلاب بخصومات حقيقية ومُعتمدة.
